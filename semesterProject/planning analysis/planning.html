Website goals:

I want this website to host useful information regarding creating art with clay. This includes learning how to make sculptures on the pottery wheel, hand-building, how to correctly use glazes, and the process of firing ceramic pieces in the kiln.

Another goal I have for this website is to inspire people to start working with clay, introduce the viewers to some of my favorite ceramic artists/artworks, and also provide accurate information so this website wil be useful.

Information and Resources:

For my content, I will need to explain the steps for throwing on the wheel in depth. Inform the viewer about the process of glazing and firing the piece in the kiln.

I will include videos and pictures from youtube and the internet for reference and to help the viewer understand fully. I will also include multiple social media accounts and my favorite ceramic artists to inspire the viewer.

I will also have a section that will list all of the tools and materials needed to start making art with clay. I will try to find materials with the cheapest prices so that this is realistic.
