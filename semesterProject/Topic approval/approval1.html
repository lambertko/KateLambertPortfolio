"Pots & Pieces"

#1-5
  1. The purpose of this website is to host useful information regarding creating art with clay. This includes learning how to make sculptures on the pottery wheel, hand-building, how to correctly use glazes, and the process of firing ceramic pieces in the kiln.
  2. I want this website to accomplish multiple things: hopefully inspire people to start working with clay, introduce the viewers to some of my favorite ceramic artists/artworks, and also provide accurate information so this website wil be useful.
  3. The intended audience I want this website to reach are people who have been searching for a new artform to learn. Also this is for people who have been interested in the work of ceramics but don't know the specifics of how to start.
  4. The content that will be included on this website will be informational paragraphs, videos, images, also links to other websites/videos and social media accounts including additional information. There will be a navigation bar to get from one page to another.
  5. My website will be unique becasue I am going to try to combine all of the information to help someone actually get started with creating ceramics, I will include what tools, materials, and even what work space they should have. I will also try to find the cheapest tools and materials to link so that this is a realistic beginner website.
